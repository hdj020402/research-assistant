# Revealing the Spatial-Molecular Fingerprint of BMP4 Expression in Breast Cancer Using ToF-SIMS and Multiple Instance Learning

**使用飞行时间二次离子质谱和多实例学习揭示乳腺癌中BMP4表达的空间-分子指纹**

## 精华总结
使用质谱成像和新型弱监督学习方法识别区分BMP4表达差异的三阴性乳腺癌的空间-分子特征，通过稳定性选择增强的多实例学习实现92%的识别准确率并发现转移预测性生物标志物。

**关键词**: 质谱成像、多实例学习、乳腺癌诊断、生物标志物发现、弱监督学习

## 摘要（英文）
Hyperspectral materials characterization techniques are becoming ubiquitous and technologically more sophisticated. There has been widespread development and application of machine/statistical learning to better understand large hyperspectral data sets. This has been remarkably fruitful across domains, yet many challenges remain. One important challenge is the utilization of large-scale data sets to uncover generalizable, population-level spatial-molecular differences between materials. We describe the application of mass spectrometry imaging and a novel weakly supervised learning method to identify the spatial-molecular signatures that differentiate two highly similar sets of triple-negative breast cancer, differing only in the expression of bone morphogenetic protein 4 (BMP4). Our method, stability selection-augmented multiple instance learning (SSAMIL): (1) accurately discriminated between the two sets of tumors (92% accuracy); (2) identified the spatial regions differentiating the tumors; and (3) identified the sparse set of molecular signatures used for classification. We validated these signatures using expert pathology annotations and additional imaging modalities, and identified a biomarker that is predictive of metastasis. We also employed high-lateral resolution delayed extraction imaging, identifying the extracellular regions as critical for distinguishing the tumors. This study demonstrates the considerable potential of this approach for medical applications (and, more generally, for materials characterization), in which spatial-spectral characterization is key.

## 摘要（中文）
超光谱材料表征技术正变得无处不在且技术上更加精进。机器学习和统计学习方法的广泛开发和应用在更好地理解大规模超光谱数据集方面取得了显著成果。这在各个领域都非常富有成效，但仍然存在许多挑战。其中一个重要的挑战是利用大规模数据集来揭示可推广的、群体水平的材料之间空间-分子差异。我们描述了质谱成像和一种新的弱监督学习方法的应用，用于识别区分两组高度相似的三阴性乳腺癌的空间-分子特征，这两组仅在骨形态发生蛋白4（BMP4）的表达上有所不同。我们的方法，稳定性选择增强多实例学习（SSAMIL）：（1）准确区分了两组肿瘤（准确率92%）；（2）识别了区分肿瘤的空间区域；（3）识别了用于分类的稀疏分子特征集。我们使用病理专家注释和额外的成像模式验证了这些特征，并识别了一个可预测转移的生物标志物。我们还采用了高横向分辨率延迟萃取成像，确定细胞外区域对于区分肿瘤至关重要。本研究展示了这种方法在医学应用中（以及更广泛的材料表征中）的巨大潜力，其中空间-光谱表征是关键。
